Track, intensity, and wind-radius data: IBTrACS v04r01 (NOAA NCEI), Western Pacific basin. Wind radii (34/50/64kt, per quadrant) are JTWC (USA agency) reports, which is why coverage starts in 2000 — systematic quadrant reporting didn't exist before then. Radius shapes interpolate between the four reported quadrant extents; treat them as an approximation, not a precise wind-field boundary.
